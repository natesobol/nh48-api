Dataset notes

  • Versioned releases with changelog and citation-ready metadata.
  • Provenance + collection methodology noted for peaks, routes, and trail segments.
  • Validation checks for elevation ranges, mileage, and geometry consistency.
  • Reproducible pipelines backed by source scripts and QA artifacts.
