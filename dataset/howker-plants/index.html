Dataset overview

Howker Ridge Plant Catalog

Structured JSON containing common and scientific names, habitat tags, elevation ranges, and seasonal notes for alpine flora observed on the Howker Ridge Trail.

  • Species profiles with bloom timing and habitat labels
  • Photo references and credit notes for catalog imagery
  • Tags for quick filtering by plant type and ecology
  • Curated notes for field identification
