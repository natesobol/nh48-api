Trail-tested notes

Experience summary

I built this guide around field photography sessions on West Bond in Twin Range - Bonds.. West Bond is a subsidiary peak of Mount Bond, with an elevation of approximately 4,540 feet.

Conditions from experience

Summer and early fall - long day hike or overnight; avoid spring mud. High - very remote, summit and ridge exposed to weather with no nearby shelters.

Planning tip

Zealand Road > Zealand Trail > Twinway > West Bond Spur - Bonds Traverse. - 19.8 - 3,950 - Extremely difficult - Traverse - point-to-point. Limited - water at Zealand Hut and streams on lower Bondcliff Trail; none on ridge itself. Lincoln Woods has large parking - fee.; Zealand Road trailhead medium - road closed in winter.
