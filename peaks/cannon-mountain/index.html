Trail-tested notes

Experience summary

I built this guide around field photography sessions on Cannon Mountain in Kinsman Range. Cannon Mountain is a 4,100-foot (1,249 m) mountain located in Grafton County, New Hampshire.

Conditions from experience

Summer and fall - black flies in spring; winter ascent common via ski slopes or Kinsman Ridge trail. Medium - summit open but a building and tower provide refuge; trail mostly wooded.

Planning tip

Kinsman Ridge Trail - via Lonesome Lake and Hi-Cannon. - 5.9 - 2,300 - Moderate - Loop - semi-loop using Hi-Cannon up, Kinsman Ridge down. Yes - Lonesome Lake on route; otherwise no flowing water on steep sections. Large parking areas at tram and Lafayette Place; very accessible - may need parking fee at tram lot during tourist season.
