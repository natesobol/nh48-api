Trail-tested notes

Experience summary

I built this guide around field photography sessions on Mount Eisenhower in Presidential Range - Southern.. Mount Eisenhower is a 4,780-foot mountain in the Presidential Range of the White Mountains.

Conditions from experience

Summer and fall - accessible in winter with proper gear via Mt. Clinton Rd ski/snowshoe. High - summit above treeline – wind and weather can be intense.

Planning tip

Edmands Path - western approach. - 6.6 - 2,750 - Moderate - Out & back Limited - seasonal small brook low on Edmands Path; otherwise none on trail. Small lot along Mt. Clinton Rd; road closed in winter - adds 2 miles walk.
