Mount Passaconaway

Mount Passaconaway guide with route details, elevation, and photos.

Elevation: 4043 ft
Prominence: 803 ft
Range: Sandwich Range
Difficulty: Difficult
Trail Type: Out & back or loop
Typical Time: 5–7 hours - via Dicey’s Mill out-and-back.
View interactive page

Overview

Gentle at start - old woods roads., then steady steep in upper mountain; well-maintained trails but rocky and rooty near top; Blueberry Ledges involve rock slabs

Location: 43.95417, -71.38111

Standard Routes

  • Dicey’s Mill Trail - standard ascent. — 9.2 mi • 2,950 ft gain • Out & back - or loop with Blueberry Ledge. • Difficult
  • Blueberry Ledge Trail > Rollins Trail > Dicey’s Mill - with Mt Whiteface. — 11.9 mi • 3,800 ft gain • Loop • Difficult
