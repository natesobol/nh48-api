Trail-tested notes

Experience summary

I built this guide around field photography sessions on Mount Field in Willey Range. Mount Field is a 4,340-foot mountain in the Willey Range of the White Mountains, located in Grafton County, New Hampshire.

Conditions from experience

Summer and fall - moderate winter hike too; Crawford Notch access year-round. Low - route largely protected in forest; summit trees break wind.

Planning tip

Willey Range Traverse - Avalon Trail > Willey Range Trail. - 10.0 - 3,400 - Difficult - Semi-loop - includes Tom & Willey. Yes - streams along Avalon & A-Z Trails at lower elevations. Park at Crawford Depot or nearby AMC Highland Center - hiker parking area.; often plenty of space
