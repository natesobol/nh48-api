Trail-tested notes

Experience summary

I built this guide around field photography sessions on Zealand Mountain in Twin Range. Zealand Mountain (sometimes called Mount Zealand) is a 4,260-foot mountain located in Grafton County, New Hampshire.

Conditions from experience

Summer and fall - Zealand Road open; also popular ski/snowshoe in winter with extra road distance. Low - mostly sheltered; caution on Zeacliff in high winds.

Planning tip

Zealand Trail > Twinway - via Zealand Hut & Zeacliff. - 11.0 - 2,300 - Difficult - Out & back - or traverse over Bonds. Yes - streams and ponds along Zealand Trail; Zealand Falls by hut. Medium-sized lot at end of Zealand Rd - paved.; road closed in snow season ~3.5 extra miles from 302
