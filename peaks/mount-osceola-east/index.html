Trail-tested notes

Experience summary

I built this guide around field photography sessions on Mount Osceola - East Peak. in Sandwich Range. Mount Osceola, East Peak (often simply called East Osceola) is a 4,156-foot mountain in the White Mountains of New Hampshire.

Conditions from experience

Summer and fall - Greeley Ponds trail is wet in spring; winter ascents possible with traction. Medium - protected in trees but rocky ridge can be slick when wet.

Planning tip

Greeley Ponds Trail → Mount Osceola Trail - via East Peak. - 7.6 - 3100 - Difficult - Out & back Limited – small brooks near trailheads; no water on the ridge Kancamagus trailhead has a small lot; Tripoli Road lot has a fee and toilets
