Conditions from experience

Webster-Jackson Trail - Jackson Branch. - 5.2 - 2,150 - Moderate - Out & back Limited - Silver Cascade Brook near trail beginning; after that, mostly dry ridge. Roadside parking by trailhead or at AMC Highland Center 0.1 mi away; usually sufficient except peak foliage
