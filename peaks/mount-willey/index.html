Experience summary

I built this guide around field photography sessions on Mount Willey in Willey Range. Mount Willey is a 4,285-foot mountain in the Willey Range of the White Mountains, situated in Grafton County, New Hampshire.

Conditions from experience

Summer and fall - steep sections have ladders – easier in snow than ice. Low - route mostly sheltered except brief ledge view.

Planning tip

Willey Range Trail - from Ethan Pond Trail via Kedron Flume. - 5.4 - 2,900 - Difficult - Out & back Yes - Kedron Brook near base; small brook by Ethan Pond Trail; no water on steep upper section. At Willey House visitor area - bathrooms, picnic tables available.; ample parking except peak foliage days
