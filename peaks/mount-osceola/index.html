Trail-tested notes

Experience summary

I built this guide around field photography sessions on Mount Osceola in Sandwich Range. Mount Osceola is a 4,340-foot (1,320 m) peak within the White Mountains of New Hampshire.

Conditions from experience

Summer and fall - Tripoli Road is closed in winter; Greeley Ponds approach is used in winter. Medium - summit exposed but a quick retreat to treeline is possible.

Planning tip

Mount Osceola Trail - Tripoli Road. - 6.4 - 2100 - Moderate - Out & back Limited – small brooks near the trailhead; no reliable water on the ridge Small lot with fee and toilets; fills quickly – overflow parking along Tripoli Road
