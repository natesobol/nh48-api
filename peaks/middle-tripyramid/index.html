Overview

Ridge between North and Middle is steep and rough but forested; Sabbaday approach is gradual then steep near ridge with some mud/stream in places

Location: 43.96471, -71.43995

Standard Routes

  • Pine Bend Brook > Mt. Tripyramid Trail - North & Middle loop. — 11.0 mi • 3,100 ft gain • Loop - via both North & Middle peaks. • Difficult
  • Sabbaday Brook Trail - longer gradual approach to Middle. — 14.0 mi • 3,000 ft gain • Out & back • Difficult - many stream crossings.
