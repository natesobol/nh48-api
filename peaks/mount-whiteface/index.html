Mount Whiteface

Blueberry Ledge Trail is moderate low, then very steep and ledgy high up - including rock scrambles.; Rollins Trail ridge mostly gentle and wooded

Elevation: 4,020 ft Prominence: 591 ft Range: Sandwich Range
