Trail-tested notes

Experience summary

I built this guide around field photography sessions on North Kinsman Mountain in Kinsman Range. North Kinsman Mountain is a 4,293-foot peak in the Kinsman Range of the White Mountains, in Grafton County, New Hampshire.

Conditions from experience

Summer and early fall - muddy in spring; winter ascents possible with traction. Medium - summit ledge exposed to wind; ridge protected.

Planning tip

Lonesome Lake Trail → Cascade Brook Trail → Fishin’ Jimmy Trail → Kinsman Ridge Trail - 9.8 - 3550 - Difficult - Out & back - continuing over South Kinsman.
