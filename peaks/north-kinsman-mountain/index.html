North Kinsman Mountain guide with route details, elevation, and photos.

Overview

Steady climb to Lonesome Lake followed by rough, rooty Fishin’ Jimmy Trail with rock steps and bolted wooden steps on steep slabs; final ridge climb is steep but manageable

Location: 44.12300, -71.73833

Standard Routes

  • Lonesome Lake Trail → Cascade Brook Trail → Fishin’ Jimmy Trail → Kinsman Ridge Trail — 9.8 mi • 3550 ft gain • Out & back - continuing over South Kinsman. • Difficult
  • Mount Kinsman Trail - from NH 116. — 10.0 mi • 3950 ft gain • Out & back • Difficult
