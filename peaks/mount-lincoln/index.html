Trail-tested notes

Experience summary

I built this guide around field photography sessions on Mount Lincoln in Franconia Range. Mount Lincoln is a 5,089-foot (1,551 m) mountain in the Franconia Range of the White Mountains.

Conditions from experience

Summer and fall - clear weather for ridge views. High - ridge highly exposed to wind, lightning in storms.

Planning tip

Franconia Ridge Loop - via Falling Waters Trail. - 8.9 - 3,900 - Very difficult - Loop - includes Mount Lafayette. Yes - waterfalls on Falling Waters Trail in lower section. Shared parking with Lafayette loop; plan for early arrival or use shuttle/overflow lots
