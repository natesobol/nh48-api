Experience summary

I built this guide around field photography sessions on Mount Waumbek in Pliny Range. Mount Waumbek is a 4,006-foot mountain in Coös County, New Hampshire.

Conditions from experience

Year-round - popular winter 4k; summer for clear Presidential views from outlook; avoid spring mud. Low - trail sheltered; only concern is typical winter cold at viewpoint.

Planning tip

Starr King Trail - via Mt Starr King. - 7.2 - 2,700 - Moderate - Out & back Limited - no major water sources on trail; an old spring near Starr King summit sometimes flows lightly, but plan to carry water. Small parking area on unpaved Starr King Rd by trailhead sign; if full, park along road without blocking driveways
