Trail-tested notes

Experience summary

I built this guide around field photography sessions on Mount Lafayette in Franconia Range. Mount Lafayette is a 5,249-foot (1,600 m) mountain at the northern end of the Franconia Range in the White Mountains of New Hampshire.

Conditions from experience

Late spring through fall - popular in summer and foliage season. High - ridge is exposed to wind, fog, thunderstorms.

Planning tip

Franconia Ridge Loop - Falling Waters & Old Bridle Path. - 8.9 - 3,900 - Very difficult - Loop - ridge traverse. Yes - stream along Falling Waters Trail; Greenleaf Hut near summit has water. Large lot at Lafayette Place but fills early; overflow parking along I-93 northbound
