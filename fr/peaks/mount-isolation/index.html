Mont Isolation

Long approach through Wilderness: muddy, many stream crossings - water hazard in wet seasons.; final Davis Path section moderate; if via Glen Boulder, significant above-treeline steep sections

Altitude: 4,004 ft Proéminence: 203 ft Chaîne: Presidential Range - Montalban Ridge.
