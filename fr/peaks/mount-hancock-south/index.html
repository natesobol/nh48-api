Mont Hancock - South Peak.

Long flat approach along the North Fork of Hancock Branch with several brook crossings, followed by a very steep climb on loose gravel to the ridge and a steep descent from South Hancock

Altitude: 4,278 ft Proéminence: 197 ft Chaîne: Pemigewasset Wilderness - Hancock Range.
